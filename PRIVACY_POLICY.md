Privacy Policy — FitCoach AI

Last updated: May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FitCoach AI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our web application and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

- Account Information: When you register or sign in using Firebase Authentication (email/password or Google Sign-In), we store basic account details such as UID, display name, email address, and profile photo URL.
- Usage and Activity Data: We collect data about your workouts (exercise selection, reps, duration, angles, calibration metadata) and analytics related to app usage to provide personalized coaching and to show progress and trends. Some values are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) for faster access and offline persistence.
- Device and Technical Data: We collect device and browser information (user agent, device capabilities), and may collect limited camera permission usage (camera stream used only for on-device pose detection/AI inference in your browser). We do not upload raw video from your camera to our backend unless explicitly described and permitted.
- Optional Inputs: If you provide meals, personal information for payments, or other optional details (age, phone, goals), we store them as part of the features you use.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Provide and personalize the Service (AI coaching, diet plan generation, voice coach cues).
- Maintain your account, show progress history, and remember preferences.
- Improve the Service through analytics and diagnostics.
- Communicate with you regarding account, support, billing, or service updates.

3. On-Device Processing and AI

- Pose detection and angle calculations are performed on-device (in the browser) using AI models to preserve privacy and performance. Only derived metrics (rep counts, calibrated angles) are stored by the app.
- Diet plan and meal analysis features call our backend AI service. When using these features, limited inputs (exercise name, rep count, meals text) are sent to the backend for processing.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on third-party providers for core functionality, including but not limited to:
- Firebase (Authentication and related services)
- Third-party AI/backend services (see project backend configuration); prompts and minimal inputs may be sent to the provider.
- Optional analytics or monitoring services.

These providers have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them before using features that rely on those services.

5. Payment & Premium

- Payment processing or simulated payment flows may involve third-party payment providers. We do not store raw payment credentials in the app. If you upgrade to Premium, we may store an indicator (e.g., `fitcoach-premium`) tied to your account.

6. Local Storage

We persist certain settings and progress locally using localStorage (daily progress, calibration data, UI preferences). Clearing your browser storage will remove that local data.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request that we delete the personal data associated with your account. For accounts created via Firebase, deleting your Firebase account will remove the account record (depending on provider retention). Contact us at the address below for manual deletion requests.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top indicates when it was last revised.
